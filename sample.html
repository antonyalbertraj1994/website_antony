PocketView: Through-Fabric Information Displays

 Antony Albert Raj Irudayaraj,Rishav Agarwal,Nikhita Joshi, Aakar Gupta, Omid Abari, Daniel Vogel It is sometimes inconvenient to take the phone out of the pocket to view information from the phone. This retrieval process can be cumbersome when holding a shopping bag and potentially dangerous while biking. So, we explore the idea of directly viewing information through the fabric of a pocket using low-resolution bright LED matrix displays.
